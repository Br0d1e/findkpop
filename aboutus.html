About FindKpop

Built by fans, for fans

What is FindKpop?

FindKpop is a K-pop deal discovery platform that helps fans find the best prices on albums, merch, and collectibles from across the internet in one place.

Our Mission

K-pop merch can be expensive and hard to track down. Our mission is to make it easier for fans worldwide to compare prices and find the best deals instantly.

Why we built it

We’re K-pop fans ourselves. We got tired of searching across multiple websites just to find the same album cheaper somewhere else — so we built a tool to solve it.

What’s next?

We’re planning to add more features like price alerts, wishlist tracking, and personalized recommendations for your favourite groups.
